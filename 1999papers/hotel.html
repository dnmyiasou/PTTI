PTTI '99 Transportation Information

The hotel is mid-way between San Diego and Los Angeles. It can be reached from five airports - Los Angeles International Airport, John Wayne in Orange Coungy, San Diego International Airport, Ontario Airport in the Riverside, CA area and Long Beach Airport. The nearest airport, John Wayne, is approximately 30 minutes from the hotel. All others are 1 to 1.5 hours away.

Avis is the official rental car agency for PTTI '99. The Avis Worldwide Discount (AWD) number for the meeting is J628452. Please use this number when calling 800-331-1600 for reservations.

United Airlines is the official airline for PTTI '99. The meeting ID code is 527ZJ. Please use this number when calling 800-521-4041 for reservations. The discounted rates are good only through December 4-12, 1999.
