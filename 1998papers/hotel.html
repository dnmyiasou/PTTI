PTTI'98 Meeting Location
Hotel and Cost Information


PTTI'98 will take place at The Hyatt Regency Hotel located in the Reston Town Center, Reston, Virginia just outside of Washington, D.C. The Reston Town Center includes a wide variety of shops and restaurants and is less than 10 minutes from Dulles Airport.
