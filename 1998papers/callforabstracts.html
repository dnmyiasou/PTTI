CALL FOR ABSTRACTS

PTTI'98 Abstract Submission

PTTI Systems and Applications 1998 focuses on ensembling multiple clocks, time transfer, new standards, Y2K, GPS week rollover, leap seconds, communications applications, computer network timing, GPS carrier phase for time transfer and navigation applications.

Submit a one page abstract (350 words or less) of your paper describing the objectives, results, conclusions and significance of your work. Please list all contributing authors and affiliations and provide contact information for the primary author, including mailing address, phone and fax numbers, and E-mail address.

E-mail (preferred - text only please), fax or mail abstracts no later than JULY 15, 1998 to the:
