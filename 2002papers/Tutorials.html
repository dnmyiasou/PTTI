PTTI 2002 Tutorials

Tutorials will take place prior to PTTI 2002 on Monday, December 2, 2002 from 0900 to 1630. The fee for the all day tutorials is $200.00. Breakfast and lunch are included. Please register using the Registration Card or On-line Registration .

The tutorial topics are as follows:

  • Clocks
    • Cesium Clocks - Mike Gavey, Datum
    • Rubidium Clocks - Bill Riley, Datum
    • Hydrogen Masers - Paul Koppang, USNO

  • Clock Statistics - David Howe, NIST

  • Timescales - Tom Parker, NIST

  • UTC - Felicitas Arias, BIPM

  • GNSS Time Transfer
    • USA - Bill Klepczynski, Consultant
    • Europe - to be announced
