PTTI 2002 Meeting Hotel and Transportation

Hotel Information


PTTI 2002 will take place at The Hyatt Regency Hotel, at Reston Town Center, located in Reston Virginia, just outside of Washington, DC. The Reston Town Center hosts a wide variety of shops and restaurants and is less than 10 minutes from Dulles International Airport.

PTTI 2002 Transportation Information

Avis has been chosen as the official car rental agency for PTTI 2002. For reservations, call 1-800-331-1600. The Avis Worldwide Discount (AWD) number for PTTI 2002 is J628452. Special meeting rates are available from November 26, 2002 through December 5, 2002.

AVIS website for PTTI 2002: https://www.avis.com/AvisWeb/html/meetings/webpage.html?1347


United Airlines has been chosen as the official airline for PTTI 2002. Call United's Meeting Desk at 1-800-521-4041; refer to Meeting Plus ID code 556XF. Fares are available November 29, 2002 through December 7, 2002.
