PTTI 2004 Meeting Hotel and Transportation

Hotel Information


PTTI 2004 will take place at the Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill, in Washington, DC. The hotel is located within steps of the U.S. Capitol, Library of Congress, Supreme Court, Smithsonian Museums, Washington Mall and Amtrak's Union Station.


Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill
(PTTI 2004 Meeting Room Block)
400 New Jersey Avenue, NW
Washington, DC 20001
202-737-1234 (Hotel Main Number/Reservations)
202-942-1512 (Fax)


When reservations are made, mention PTTI 2004 to receive the group sleeping room rate. The PTTI rate is available from December 5 through December 10, 2004. Check-in time is 1500 (3:00 PM EST) and check-out time is Noon. Rooms are held until 1600 (4:00 PM EST) if not guaranteed for late arrival by a credit card or one night's deposit. The block of reserved rooms will be released November 5 so reserve early. If rooms are available after that date, reservations will be accepted at the group rate. If you leave before your scheduled departure date without informing the Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill, you will be charged an early departure fee.

Reservations for the Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill can be made by:
  • Phone
    • Call the Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill at 888-421-1442 or 202-737-1234
  • Mail
    • Mail the Hotel Registration Card to the Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill at the address above.
  • Fax
    • Fax the Hotel Registration Card to the Hyatt Regency Hotel on Capitol Hill at 202-942-1512
  • On-line


PTTI 2004 Transportation Information

Avis is the official car rental agency for PTTI 2004. The Avis Worldwide Discount (AWD) number for the meeting is J628452. Please call 800-331-1600 to make reservations. The rates are available from November 29, 2004 through December 16, 2004.

United Airlines is the official airline for PTTI 2004. Call 1-800-521-4041 to book your reservations. The PTTI 2004 Meeting ID code is 556XF. The rates are for Washington, DC from December 3-12, 2004.
