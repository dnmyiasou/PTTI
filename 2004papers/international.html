Attention International Attendees


Starting October 1, 2003, each Visa Waiver Program (VWP) traveler must present a machine-readable passport (MRP) at the U.S. port of entry to enter the U.S. without a visa, otherwise a nonimmigrant visa is required. This change includes all categories of passports, when the traveler is seeking to enter the U.S.

For more detailed information, contact your country's passport office.

For further information see the following U.S. Department of State websites:

http://travel.state.gov/vwp/

http://travel.state.gov/visa/
