PTTI 2004 Tutorials


Tutorials will take place prior to PTTI 2004 on Monday, December 6, 2004 from 0830 to 1645. The fee for the all day tutorials is $250.00. Breakfast and lunch are included.

For detailed information regarding the tutorials, please contact Mrs. Rachel Gross via voice mail at 202-404-4011 or e-mail at gross@juno.nrl.navy.mil.

PTTI 2004 Tutorials Schedule

    0730 - 0830 Breakfast
    0830 - 0930 History of Clocks and GPS - Dr. Joe White, U.S. Naval Research Laboratory
    0930 - 1030 History of Galileo - Dr. Eduardo Detoma, SEPA
    1030 - 1045 Intermission
    1045 - 1215 Crystal Technology - Dr. John Vig, U.S. Army
    1215 - 1330 Lunch
    1330 - 1430 GPS and All Its Applications - Dr. Hugo Fruehauf, FEI-Zyfer, Incorporated
    1430 - 1530 Satellite Time and Frequency Transfer - Christine Hackman, JILA, University of Colorado
    1530 - 1545 Intermission
    1545 - 1645 Frequency Stability Analysis of Oscillators and Clocks - David Howe, National Institute of Standards and Technology
