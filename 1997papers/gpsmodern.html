GPS Modernization - Request for User Input
Wednesday, December 3
7:00 pm to 9:00 pm


The March 1996 Presidential Decision Directive on U.S. GPS Policy recognized GPS as an integral part of the "Global Information Infrastructure" with a broad range of military, civil, commercial and scientific interests, both national and international. The U.S. government is seeking civilian sector input into a Future Worldwide Positioning, Velocity, and Timing System Capstone Requirements Document to address the near, mid, and far term modernization of the GPS.

A side-session will be held December 3, from 7:00 pm until 9:00 pm during the 1997 Precise Time and Time Interval (PTTI) Systems and Applications Meeting to gather additional timing requirements regarding the GPS Modernization.
