SPECIAL NOTICE *** SLEEPING ROOMS *** SPECIAL NOTICE

PTTI'97 Hotel Notice


Unfortunately, this year's PTTI meeting has run into a stumbling block. The Sheraton at Long Beach does have rooms available for PTTI attendees from 29 November - 3 December. However, right on the heels of the PTTI meeting, NIKE is holding a meeting at the convention center in Long Beach and has booked almost all of the available hotel rooms throughout the city (including the Sheraton) for the nights of 4-6 December.

For those of you planning to stay the weekend and then flying to Boulder, CO, to attend the TWSTT meeting, PTTI was unable to block sufficient rooms for those nights due to the lateness in the announcement for that meeting.

Never fear, there are other options available. The Crown Plaza Hotel in Redondo Beach has set aside a block of rooms for the nights of 4-6 December at the government per diem rate of $97. You may call for reservations at 310-318-8888 and request a room from the PTTI room block.

We do apologize for the inconvenience.
