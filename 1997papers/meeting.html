PTTI'97 Meeting Information

PTTI'97 will take place at the Sheraton Long Beach Hotel located in Long Beach, California. The special sleeping room rate, which is the current government rate, tax inclusive, will be available to all PTTI'97 attendees.
PTTI'97 Registration fees
Full Registration postmarked on or BEFORE November 10 ...................... $130
Full Registration postmarked AFTER November 10 ................................... $150

Full-time Student .............................................................................................. $50
Full-time Retirees ............................................................................................. $50

Tuesday, December 2, Dinner/Reception
Guest Tickets ..................................................................................................... $25


The full registration fee includes entry to all sessions, the exhibits, a copy of the Proceedings, and the welcoming reception on Tuesday evening. Attendees, including presenters, must register and receive a badge. The badge must be worn for admission to all sessions and functions. Visitor badges for the exhibits only should be obtained at the registration desk. Exhibits are open to everyone.
