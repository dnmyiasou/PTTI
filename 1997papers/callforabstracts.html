CALL FOR ABSTRACTS

PTTI'97 Abstract Submission

PTTI'97 will focus on timing systems, design, integration and applications. Precise time plays a significant role in telecommunications, networks, the power industry and the military.

Submit a one page abstract (350 words or less) of your paper describing the objectives, results, conclusions and significance of your work. Please list all contributing authors and affiliations, providing contact information for the primary author, including mailing address, phone and fax numbers, and E-mail address.

Mail, fax or e-mail (text only) abstracts by AUGUST 1, 1997 to:

Technical Program Chairman
Mr. Don Mitchell
TrueTime, Inc.
2835 Duke Court
Santa Rosa, CA 95407

Authors will be notified of acceptance by September 15, 1997.


PTTI '97 will address Timing Systems and Applications
Session Topics include:

  • Network and Telecommunications Timing and Synchronization
    Network Time Protocol (NTP), T1/E1, SONET, ATM, Gateways,
    PCS, Digital Cellular, Satellite Ground Stations
  • Time Transfer Techniques
    Global Positioning System (GPS), Two Way Satellite Time Transfer (TWSTT),
    GLONASS, Range Countdown Systems, Range Timing Upgrade
  • Specifications and Standards
    Procurement specifications: Manufacturer, End User
    Standards: IEEE, EIA, CCIT, IRIG, ISO, ICD
  • International Timing and Synchronization
  • Panel Discussions:
    - Systems Design and Integration
    - Leap Second, Leap Year, GPS Week Rollover, Year 2000

PTTI '97 Final Paper Submission

Final papers must be submitted by December 2, 1997.

The PTTI'97 proceedings will be mailed to attendees in mid 1998.
