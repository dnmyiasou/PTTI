PTTI OBJECTIVES

The Objectives of the PTTI Applications and Planning Meeting are to:

  • Disseminate and Coordinate PTTI information at the user level.

  • Review present and future PTTI requirements.

  • Inform Government engineers, technicians, and managers of precise time and frequency technology and its problems.

  • Provide an opportunity for an active exchange of new technology associated with PTTI.


This year, PTTI 96 sessions will address:

  • International Activities of PTTI Technology

  • PTTI Time Transfer Techniques

  • PTTI Applications in Telecommunications

  • Operational PTTI

  • Timing Systems Performance


Tutorials will be offered prior to PTTI'96

  • Timing 101 - Fundamentals

  • Timing 201 - Advanced Concepts

WELCOME TO PTTI'96


KEYNOTE SPEAKER

Giovanni Busca, Observatoire Cantonal de Neuchâtel, Switzerland

Impact of Atomic Clocks on Navigation, Communication and Sciences

SUMMARY: An analysis of the development of atomic clocks during the last decade shows that applications, particularly where related to GPS, have grown at a rate much faster than clock development itself. However, improved clock performances are expected to be determinant for future applications in navigation, communication and sciences. This subject is illustrated by presenting possible future development trends with particular emphasis on space applications.
