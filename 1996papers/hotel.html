PTTI'96 Meeting Location
Hotel and Cost Information


PTTI'96 will take place at The Hyatt Regency Hotel located in the Reston Town Center, Reston, Virginia just outside of Washington, D.C. The Reston Town Center includes a wide variety of shops and restaurants and is less than 10 minutes from Dulles Airport.

The Hyatt Regency Hotel at Reston Town Center
(PTTI'96 Meeting Room Block)
1800 Presidents Street
Reston, Virginia
Tel: 703-709-1234
Fax: 703-709-2291

If reservations are made by telephone, be sure to mention PTTI'96 to receive the special sleeping room rate, which is the current government rate, tax inclusive, for the Washington, D.C. area. The block of reserved rooms will be released November 17, 1996, so register early to ensure the PTTI'96 rate. If rooms are available, reservations will be accepted after that date at the group rate. The PTTI'96 rate is available November 30 through December 8, 1996. Cancellation of the room must be made 48 hours prior to the arrival date.
