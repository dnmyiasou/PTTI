PTTI 2003 Meeting Registration Information

PTTI 2003 Registration fees
Full Registration postmarked on or BEFORE November 7 ...................... $300
Full Registration postmarked AFTER November 7 ................................... $350

Full-time Student .......................................................................................... $50*
Full-time Retirees ......................................................................................... $50*
*Does not include Proceedings or Reception

PTTI 2003 Tutorials .................................................................................... $250

The full registration fee includes entry to all technical sessions, the exhibit area, a copy of the Proceedings on CD-ROM, the welcoming reception, and meal functions. Guest tickets will need to be purchased for the meal functions. Prices to be provided at a later date.

The registration fee for students and full-time retirees includes entry to the scientific sessions and the exhibit area only. It does not include a copy of the Proceedings on CD-ROM, or entry to the meal functions.

Attendees, including presenters, must register and receive a badge. The badge must be worn for admission to all sessions and functions. Visitor badges for the exhibits only should be obtained at the registration desk. Exhibits are open to everyone.
