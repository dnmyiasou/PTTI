PTTI 2003 Tutorials


Tutorials will take place prior to PTTI 2003 on Monday, December 1, 2003 from 0800 to 1715. The fee for the all day tutorials is $250.00. Breakfast and lunch are included.

For detailed information regarding the tutorials, please contact Ken Senior, 202-767-2043, or by E-mail: Ken.Senior@nrl.navy.mil.

    0800 - 0930 Crystal Oscillators - John Vig, CEC U.S. Army
    0930 - 1030 Atomic Frequency Standards - Robert Tjoelker, JPL
    1030 - 1045 Intermission
    1045 - 1145 Clocks and Timing for Telecommunications - George Shaton, Eagle Alliance
    1145 - 1300 Lunch
    1300 - 1400 Clock Statistics - Don Percival, University of Washington
    1400 - 1500 Clock Measurement - Sam Stein, Timing Solutions Corporation
    1500 - 1515 Intermission
    1515 - 1615 Timescales - Tom Parker, NIST
    1615 - 1715 Time/Frequency Transfer - Judah Levine, NIST
